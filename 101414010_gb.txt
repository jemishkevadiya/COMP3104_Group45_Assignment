George Brown College is a public college in Toronto, Ontario. There are 3 mainly campuses in GTA area - Casa Loma, St. James & Waterfront. There is also a Toronto Metropolian University campus located at 99 Gerrard St. E. 
Our current President is Dr. Gervan Fearon who took the position is 2021. The college offers diploma programs, advanced diploma programs, as well as degree programs.
GBC offers courses in
Arts, Design & Information Technology
Business
Community Services & Early Childhood
Construction & Engineering Technologies
Health Sciences
Hospitality & Culinary Arts
