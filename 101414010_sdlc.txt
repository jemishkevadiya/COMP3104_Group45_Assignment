DevOps ia an integral part in Software development cycle. 
As the name suggests it combines two roles Developer(Dev) and Operations(Qps). 
It is important to have knowlegde about DevOps as it helps us streamline development process by 
automating repetitive tasks, improving collaboration etc. Continuous Integration(CI) and Continuous Deployment(CD) tools are more reliable to 
execute tasks faster. Moreover,DevOps enables organizations to scale their software infrastructure 
with the containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
